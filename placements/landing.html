About Us

Welcome to PlacePro, your go-to platform for tracking and analyzing campus placements. We aim to provide students with insights into placement trends, helping them prepare better for job opportunities. Our platform allows users to explore placement records, including details about companies, job roles, and salary packages. With an interactive dashboard, students can analyze placement patterns based on CGPA, skills, and hiring trends. To make the experience even more engaging, we have integrated an AI-powered chatbot that answers common placement-related queries, ensuring students get the guidance they need instantly. PlacePro is designed to be simple, insightful, and user-friendly, offering colleges and students a seamless way to monitor and understand placement outcomes.
